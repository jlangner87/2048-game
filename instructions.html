How to play 2048

1.) Objective: combine titles to reach 2048.

2.) Tiles are combined when moved in the direction of an adjacent tile with the same number.

3.) Move tiles left by pressing 'J'

4.) Move tiles right by pressing 'K'

5.) Move tiles down by pressing 'M'

6.) Move tiles up by pressing 'I'

7.) You lose when no more moves can be made.

8.) You can continue playing after reaching 2048 and try to get high score!
